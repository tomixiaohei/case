Sound system design of cinemas

         Dolby stereo film since 1995 was introduced into China, the film stereo technology to get rapid development, especially the digital movie show, has greatly promoted China's film science and technology progress. Nowadays, most of the urban theaters in China have been transformed into SR stereophonic or digital stereo audios. The acoustics system of the film academy is a wide range, which includes the architectural acoustics planning in the cinema and all the sound system and its planning.

         The general small hall cinema's speakers are placed in front of the left and right speakers, one or two speakers, 2 or more low voice, according to the area to match, 3 sides around four or more, and then coiled 2. Basically a 200 person to 300 people to listen to such an organization can, a little larger to increase the power of a single sound box. In the cinema today are relatively large, treble, and above separately on the sound box body, a unit generally has 2-4, more than 15 inches in diameter, and a box with no net cover, the speaker coil front panel are downward sloping. Why do you make such a plan? It can be analyzed according to the features of the cinema's sound.

         One, the soprano horn
         Since the modern cinema has realized the sound of stereophonic sound, the cinema stereo is made up of the left, middle and right, and the corresponding sound box is also placed in the silvery. This placed, film screen width exceeds 10 meters, such as only two stereo set left and right, so that the middle field was weak, and the characters in the film speak generally in the middle of the best listening position after the shift, reduce, and therefore, the picture image separation, it is necessary to increase road stereo cinema at the same time in order to make the image information and pictures together, the speaker should be placed on the silver surface, to give the audience the feeling is like a natural; in addition, the speaker placement height are also necessary to meet the common requirements of sound and picture, the sound box horn at the height of the screen height of the 2/3, in order to meet the requirements of sound character. Because in the theater, left, and right in the sound box, to cover the entire audience hall, so the high frequency unit, especially the tweeter horn dispersion is necessary for effective control, the general requirement is 90 degrees, 40 degrees vertical, at the same time as the silver box under dark, screen due to occlusion a greater loss of up to 10dB, also called horn dispersion angle is larger, to expand the sound effect.

         Two, low sound unit
         The cinema sound fidelity is very high, all kinds of sound requirements can restore nature in the whole audio range, as far as possible the foil film atmosphere, so the theater speaker voice requirements dive low, such as the sound of the guns, the engine roar, to dive to about 35Hz, and there is considerable energy, in the jargon I was still called the time low, these two aspects are required low caliber unit, and 2-4 unit stack operations. Only in this way can we meet the requirements of high fidelity. A good time to reproduce, the stereo multiplex signal, a signal through a low road time, low sound box time reduction, low sound pressure level in the digital stereo, to be higher than 10dB full band, a low rated power unit is 600 watts. In 500 theaters, the power of the low - sound unit is up to 4000~6000 watts.

         Three, the low sound box has no net cover
         As mentioned before, the cinemas are all placed in the dark. In order to reduce the attenuation of the sound signals and to consider the safety of the sound, it is not necessary to set the sound box net cover.

         Four. The front panel of the coiled sound box is slanted down
         The general theater coiled speakers are placed in the auditorium from 2/3 after the side wall and the rear wall, at intervals of 4 meters, height 4 meters, taking into account the projection direction for the audience's ear sound around, left and right points around the coil, to reach the audience is only the direct sound. The audience can distinguish surround sound direction, so the front panel speakers are made of coiled downward sloping. There are also some technical requirements that make the theater speakers different from the stage speakers, which are:
              The 1. frequency points are selected at a low level. When the speakers with two frequency division are selected, the frequency points of the movie speakers are generally 500Hz or 630Hz, while the general stage boxes get higher 1.2KHz. This is mainly to consider the film's dialogue to avoid the frequency point, and increase the clarity;
              2. the power divider of electronic frequency divider as far as possible will produce greater distortion. As a high fidelity cinema sound box, electronic frequency division mode is generally used.
              3.The combination of speaker power and power amplifier in the use of movie speakers, in order to consider the dynamic range and the sound quality and safety of speakers, the power of driving power amplifier is 1.5~2 times of the rated power of speakers.

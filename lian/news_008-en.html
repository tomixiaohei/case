The concrete cause of the damage to the sound box of the sound box

         In many professional occasions, such as studio, theatre, conference room, song and dance hall, etc., often present the image of the medium and high tone units that burn the sound box. In the sound equipment, the soprano unit is very important. But because of the characteristics of its layout, it is often burned down, not only the loss of the economy will also damage the performance, and even make it impossible to perform normal performance. The main reasons are as follows:

         1. The equipment of the sound box and power amplifier is not reasonable
         It is usually considered that the output power of the power amplifier is too large to make up the damage of the high sound unit, but it is not true. In fact, the speaker can usually accept large signal 3 times extra power shock, instantaneous peak impact several times more acceptable to additional power and will not constitute damage. Therefore, the condition of a high power amplifier with a high power amplifier is rarely presented. It is well known that usually there are many loudspeakers in the sound box, and the power allocated by different loudspeakers according to the frequency division points is different. The extra power of speakers is usually the largest pink noise power of professional speakers, that is to say, the extra power of speakers is the power of analog signals, which is acceptable by pink noise or broadband. A two frequency speech box with a frequency division point of 1.6kHz and an additional power of 100W. When extra power is applied, the low voice unit can be allocated to the output power of 78W, while the tweeter unit is allocated to 22W only. Therefore, it can accept the 100W's pink noise power or the usual program signal power, and when using the 100W single frequency signal to test, regardless of the high pitch and low voice unit, it can be damaged. If the power distribution is not appropriate, it will be very simple to form the damage of the soprano unit.
         Through the above analysis, we can clearly understand that in the sound system, the important reason for the loudspeaker burning high pitch unit is that the power of the power amplifier is too small. The signal sent by the power amplifier is the cutting signal, which causes the damage of the sound box. Therefore, when we are equipped with audio, we must establish the correct knowledge, reasonably allocate the power, avoid the power amplifier sending the clipping signal, and damage the high and medium pitch loudspeaker units. During the planning of the sound system, the power amplifier and the planning power of the sound are matched with the above guidelines. In the practical operation, the equipment in each link should be applied rationally so as to protect both the equipment and the sound system.

         2. The equalizer is not properly debugged
         The adjustment of the equalizer is also crucial. The frequency equalizer is designed to compensate for the various disadvantages of the indoor sound field and the uneven frequency of the loudspeaker. It should be debugged with the practical spectrum analyzer or other instruments. The transmission frequency characteristics after debugging should be flat in a certain range. Many voice players who don't have the knowledge of sound are debugging at will, and even a large number of people have raised the high frequency and low frequency of the equalizer, forming a "V" shape. If these frequencies and pitch frequencies are increased by more than 10dB, the phase distortion of the equalizer should not only be severely stained with the music, but also easily form the loudspeaker unit, which is also the main reason for burning the loudspeaker. Of course, the planning of sound system should be summarized according to the actual situation, such as the size of the place, the use and the conditions of building sound. We should determine the maximum successive sound pressure level according to the practical application conditions, and then determine the maximum SPL value of the loudspeaker.

         3, the volume is not properly adjusted
         Some use the attenuator amplifier is arranged in the -6dB level, -10dB, 80% - 70% volume knob, and usually the range, by enlarging the mixer input to reach the appropriate volume, that the power amplifier of a room, the speaker is safe, this is the fault of the practice. The power amplifier is the input signal attenuation attenuation knob, if the power amplifier input attenuation of 6dB, which means to adhere to the equal volume, mixer or before necessary output 6dB voltage to 1 times higher, dynamic margin input, commonly known as "head space", will be cut off half. At this time, if there is a sudden big signal, it will be early 6dB to make the mixer output overload, showing the cutting wave. Although the amplifier has no overload, but the input is the clipping waveform, high frequency weight, not only high sound distortion, it is able to burn the high tone unit.
         Through the above analysis, we can clearly understand that in the sound system, the important reason for the loudspeaker burning high pitch unit is that the power of the power amplifier is too small. The signal sent by the power amplifier is the cutting signal, which causes the damage of the sound box. Therefore, when we are equipped with audio, we must establish the correct knowledge, reasonably allocate the power, avoid the power amplifier sending the clipping signal, and damage the high and medium pitch loudspeaker units. During the planning of the sound system, the power amplifier and the planning power of the sound are matched with the above guidelines.

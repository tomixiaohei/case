The acoustics design of the theater

         The theatre is a special performing field including the opera house and the National Theatre. The acoustical demand of the theater is similar to that of the concert hall, and there are some special needs. The theatre venue is a music hall, more specialized very large audience, unconnected with the stage and the stage generally has the box mouth, have Orchestra, the audience have a box. The reverberation time is long, but considering the opera and the dialogue, it is necessary to take the sound of the language into consideration. Therefore, the reverberation time is shorter than that of the concert hall. Appropriate sound diffusion to avoid echo interference. Acoustic orchestra music needs to balance the inner voices. At present, the reasons for the poor sound quality of many theaters are that the technical aspects of acoustic planning are unreasonable, most of which are due to the fact that the main purpose and performing methods of the theater were not initially clear when planning. For example, a movie is the first use of the auditorium, but with music, dance and other requirements that made acoustic indicators, growth is not suitable for the use of; again, first performed by the actual sound system as, but with natural sound performance planning, counterproductive. This kind of example is too numerous to enumerate, so it is necessary to think of it.

         The sound quality planning of the theater
         The sound quality of the theater is usually lower than that of the concert hall. However, the workload and difficulty of the acoustic planning is far greater than the concert hall, which shows the following aspects.
         ①There is a confluence space between the auditorium and the huge stage. The listeners and actors are at different places. They must learn to handle at the same time, while the auditorium and Concert Hall in concert hall are in the same space.
         ②The theater usually needs to meet the needs of multiple functions, and even the professional theater should consider the possibility of "one - purpose and multi - use". The same hall is acoustically adapted to the acoustic needs of different repertoires, which is more difficult than a single use concert hall.
         ③The dialogue power in drama and local opera performance is much lower than that in singing and orchestra playing, while the opera performance is how to pass through the voice wall of the band.
         ④The theatre audience hall should consider both the natural sound and the sound expansion of two kinds of performing methods. The concert hall is usually planned only by natural sound performance.
         ⑤In the modern multi function theater auditorium, the layout adjustment, whether planning, construction and control system or professional cooperation, is much more complicated than the concert hall. In the construction process, it is easy to have problems in acoustic effect.
         ⑥The mechanical parts of the theater and the automatic control system are very technical, and they usually have to entrust the stage professional planning department to complete it. And the concert hall is a little bit more.
         ⑦In the subjective evaluation of sound quality, the literature of the concert hall is the most. In recent decades, the evaluation and study of the acoustical quality of the hall is primarily aimed at the establishment of the concert hall. For the opera performance. In particular, the local opera in China, how to carry out the quality of sound quality planning lack of ready-made information, need to be further studied. To sum up, because the theater acoustics planning is very difficult, so the demand for architects in the planning process in the professional in close collaboration, communication between architectural design and acoustics for the purpose of planning, in order to make the construction planning and disposal of acoustic organically, to create a perfect sound modern theatre.

         Key points to avoid the acoustical defects of the theater
         The acoustical defects in the theater auditorium are primarily acoustic aggregation and echo. Because the audience is always arranged in the middle of the stage, the rear wall is generally concave. In order to prevent the acoustic aggregation caused by the rear wall, porous material is usually used for the sound absorption of the back wall. The diffusion layout can also be used to eliminate the obvious acoustic aggregation. A pair of parallel side walls, if not treated with sound, will surely produce a vibrant echo. But if the sound source is not in the audience hall, but on the stage, even if there is a trembling echo, it will not affect the application. Generally, the theater of the mirror frame stage, the sound source is always on the stage, therefore, do not have to think much of the vibrate echo. For the audience hall with extended stage and intermediate stage, the situation is quite different. Because the sound source (actor) enters the auditorium and vibrate echoes, the defect will be obvious. In this case, the parallel side wall must be dealt with by the acoustics. The method is to destroy the parallel surface or add the sound absorbing material. It can also play the same role in planning the falling box or diffusion layout on the side wall, but the porous sound absorption material should be used as little as possible. As for the elimination of echo, it should be considered when it is determined in the form of leveling and section, and the sound absorbing material is set in the place where the echo is easily produced.

         Theater sound expansion planning
         Planning to two aspects of theater acoustics and sound solve the concert band fullness and lyrics clarity problem. The performance of the opera is the only way of performing natural sound. The troupe on the actor's training of professional choreography is also planning to set the basis of natural sound performance. Therefore, the audience hall of the demand theatre is designed by natural sound performance. The opera does not use sound expansion, but a high quality sound expansion system is still needed in the theater.
         1. The necessity of setting up sound amplifying system in theaters
              ① The sound of the effect in the plot.
              ② Before and after the performance, music and notice are played for the audience hall and the rest hall, and the performance is played in the lounge.
              ③ The demand for stage supervision.
              ④ The theater is used for other uses, such as the need for sound expansion in modern musical performances, films and meetings.
         2、the other functions of the sound amplifying system ①artificial reverberation function.
              ② performance effect.
              ③ replay the function of the sound source.
              ④ Noise suppression function.
              ⑤ Pressure limit maintenance function.
              ⑥ The stage monitor function. In order to meet the needs of high fidelity professional performance, the sound expansion system should have enough power reserve.
         The Beijing theatre, for example, is a theatrical performance dominated by theatrical performances and for conferences, stereophonic films and natural sound conditions. It holds 1000 spectators with an effective volume of 5440m3. The primary acoustical planning targets of the theatre audience hall are as follows.
         (1) if the reverberation time and reverberation amplitude modulation, stereo video conference (500Hz) the reverberation time is 0.9s; the natural sound of music needs 1.2s; drama, local opera performances for 1 ~ 1.1s. According to the above demand, the adjustable reverberation amplitude is determined to be 0.3s. Considering that low frequency adjustable amplitude is lower than middle and high frequency, as well as the actual needs of opera and music performance, low frequency increases 1.2 times.
         (2) the inhomogeneity of the sound field is not more than 3.0dB in both natural sound performance and the application of sound amplifying system.

         The live platform is a new position

Under the impetus of the net red economy and the mobile social network, the broadcast platform of the network broke out in an all-round way. Whether it is a game, film and television industry, or a concert performance, all in the use of live platform. Under the trend of Internet development, traditional culture began to bring different cultural experience to audiences with the help of live broadcast platform. There are many traditional art and traditional culture anchors on the big live platforms. From January this year, Cai Yang, the chief of the Central National Orchestra, began to play the erhu on a live platform. Meanwhile, Cai Yang will also popularize the knowledge of Erhu for the netizens in live broadcast. In two months, Cai Yang has done more than ten times of live broadcast, the most time has about 200000 netizens online.          "Ring powder" is not easy under the pressure of competition
         Public information shows that as of June 2016, the number of online live broadcast platforms in China is nearly 300. It is estimated that the online platform will increase to 400 over the whole year, and the scale of the market will reach 50 billion yuan. The scale of the online live user is 325 million, accounting for 45.8% of the total number of netizens. Large scale users, broadcast platform competition intensified, while the public tired of the "Red Net", the user demand into the segmentation period, broadcast platform content is constantly enriched, covering all fields of life, movies, variety, delicacy etc.. Despite the "Chinese poetry conference", "I am in the Imperial Palace to repair cultural relics" and other variety shows, film and television dramas have made traditional culture back to the audience's vision. How to "circle powder" is still a long-term process.

         Content innovation is the key to development
         The day before, the general office of the State Council issued "on the implementation of the Chinese traditional culture inheritance and development project opinion", proposed that by 2025, Chinese traditional culture inheritance and development of a basic system. The support of the state from the strategic level to the dissemination of traditional culture is undoubtedly conducive to the development and dissemination of the traditional culture in the future. The opera, opera and other traditional cultural performers, through their re interpretation of works through technical means of vertical inheritance to young live broadcast audience, also in expanding the traditional culture of the audience, so that more viewers attention to the rise of traditional culture. In the view of Chinese Creative Industry Research Center Director Zhang Jingcheng, in the past the traditional cultural heritage is the word of mouth or by the way of text communication. Nowadays, in the era of information explosion, traditional culture, by means of new means and technologies, strengthens its influence from contents and channels, which is both the trend and necessity of the Internet era. However, the transmission process of traditional culture is not a way to spread, but a dynamic way. The way of sermons inheritance is obviously not in line with the current development trend. "The Internet and the development of new media today, the spread effect can be tens or even hundreds of times to expand. At the same time, the traditional culture is combined with the current popular culture to carry forward the traditional culture. Zhang Jingcheng said.
